Hot Dog Noodle Soup

A classic recipe with a twist.

Ingredients

  • 1 can condensed cream of chicken soup
  • 1 can condensed cream of mushroom soup
  • 3 cans chicken broth
  • 1 package of Oscar Mayer Wieners, cut into bite-size pieces
  • 2 teaspoons onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon seasoning salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 2 packages frozen egg noodles

Directions

  1. In a large pot, mix the cream of chicken soup, cream of mushroom soup, chicken broth, and hot dogs
  2. Season with onion powder, seasoning salt, and garlic powder
  3. Bring to a boil, and stir in the noodles
  4. Reduce heat to low, and simmer for 20 to 30 minutes

Hot Dog Stew

A warm meal for winter times!

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 packages of Oscar Mayer Wieners, cut into bite-size pieces
  • 4 cups water
  • Salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 6 white potatoes, cut into bite-size pieces
  • 1 pound carrots, thickly sliced
  • 4 ribs of celery, thickly sliced
  • 1 cup diced onion
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons water

Directions

  1. Pour 4 cups water into a pot; add salt and pepper. Bring liquid to a boil
  2. Stir potatoes, carrots, celery, and onion into the water; cook until all vegetables are tender, about 30 minutes
  3. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat; cook hot dogs in hot oil until desired consistency. Stir hot dogs into the pot
  4. Whisk cornstarch into 2 tablespoons of water; stir into the liquid in the pot. Continue cooking until the liquid has thickened, about 10 minutes

Hot Dog Ice Cubes

Hot dog ice cubes are perfect to have on hand whether you're adding it to a mild cocktail, punch, or simply adding flavor to your water.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups of water
  • 1 package of Oscar Mayer Wieners

Directions

  1. Bring water to a boil in a pot. Place hot dogs in boiling water for 5 minutes. Pour hot dog water in ice cube tray
  2. Cool to room temperature before placing in freezer to freeze for 3 hours
  3. (Optional) Let hot dogs cool then serve
